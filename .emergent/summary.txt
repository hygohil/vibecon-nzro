-   **Onboarding (JOIN):** A workflow to register their name, village, land details, and UPI ID.
-   **Claim Submission (CLAIM):** A workflow to submit tree count, species, date, location, and photos.
-   **Status Check:** A command to get claim status and payout estimates.
